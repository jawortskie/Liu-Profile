THE GENERL'S CUP 2024

It was truly a dashing moment to be part of this celebration. The General's Cup 2024 is a month-long celebration held at Northwestern Mindanao State College of Science and Technology. Throughout the month of October, many students across different departments gather for a friendly competition, honoring athletes and socio-cultural talents aiming to provide exposure to hidden skills and talents.

As a student of Animation and Multimedia Arts, I developed my skills through events and projects like this. Garnering experiences and exposure to such events made my outputs more outstanding each day.
